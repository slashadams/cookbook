# Banana Tempura

Warm, crispy battered banana — simple, fast, and a crowd-pleaser.

## Info

- **Yield:** Per banana
- **Time:** ~10 min
- **Oil temp:** ~350°F

## Ingredients

- Bananas, sliced into bite-sized pieces
- Tempura batter (see notes)
- Oil for deep frying
- Vanilla ice cream
- Powdered sugar
- Chocolate syrup
- Diced fresh strawberries

## Instructions

1. Slice bananas into bite-sized pieces.
2. Heat oil to ~350°F.
3. Prepare tempura batter per package directions (see notes).
4. Nest the batter bowl over another bowl of ice water to keep it cold.
5. Working a few at a time, dip banana pieces into the batter. Let excess drip off.
6. Carefully lower into hot oil. Fry for about 60 seconds until the batter is golden and crisp.
7. Remove and drain on a paper towel.
8. Plate with a scoop of ice cream. Dust with powdered sugar, drizzle chocolate syrup, and top with diced strawberries.

## Notes

Same batter and technique as [cheesecake tempura](cheesecake-tempura.md) — no need to pre-chill the banana. See that recipe for detailed notes on tempura batter, ice bath technique, and frying tips.
